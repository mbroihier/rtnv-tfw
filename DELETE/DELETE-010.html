Test Case DELETE-010
Title Pressing "Delete", deletes the stock displayed from the portfolio
Objective

This test case is intended to verify the following implied or specified requirements:

  • When the delete button is pressed, rtnv shall remove the persistent copy of the stock referenced by the ticker symbol in the display.

This test case verifies that pressing the delete key when a portfolio entry is displayed removes that entry from the portfolio.
Setup
Actions
  1. Delete the app from the target device.
  2. Compile and load rtnv onto the target Android device.
  3. Verify the compile completes with no errors.
  4. Verify the installation completes with no errors.
  5. Select the "Ticker Symbol" field and enter the ticker value of AAPL.
  6. Select the "Number of Shares" field and enter the value 200.
  7. Press the "Enter" button.
  8. Select the "Ticker Symbol" field and enter the ticker value of INTC.
  9. Select the "Number of Shares" field and enter the value 500.
  10. Press the "Enter" button.
  11. Press the "Next" button and verify that the symbol AAPL is displayed with 200 shares and that a non zero value is displayed for the last closing price of the stock.
  12. Press the "Delete" button and verify that the screen is "blanked".
  13. Press the "Next" button and verify that the symbol INTC is displayed with 500 shares and that a non zero value is displayed for the last closing price of the stock.
  14. Press the "Delete" button and verify that the screen is "blanked".
Expected Pressing delete will delete entries specified in the ticker symbol field from the portfolio.
Results PASSED
Cleanup
  • Unnecessary
Requirements RTNV-PR-DELETE-010
